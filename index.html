About Us

Christ-Centered Hope for At-Risk Youth

Youth Crusaders exists to rescue, restore, and renew at-risk and incarcerated youth through Christ-centered mentorship. We meet young people in some of the hardest moments of their lives and walk alongside them with the love, truth, and hope of the Gospel.

Rooted in the promise of Isaiah 61 — to bring good news to the broken and freedom to the captive — we believe no young person is beyond the reach of God's grace. Through consistent relationships and faithful presence, we help youth find a new path forward.

What We Do

Showing Up Where We're Needed Most

01

Youth Care Center

We hold regular services at the Youth Care Center, bringing worship, encouragement, and the message of the Gospel to youth in detention.

02

United Methodist Youth Home

We provide ongoing programming and mentorship at the United Methodist Youth Home, building lasting relationships that point young people toward restoration.

03

Powered by Volunteers

Everything we do is made possible by faithful volunteers who give their time and hearts to mentor, lead, and love the youth we serve.
